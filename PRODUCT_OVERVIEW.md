# MediPay - Executive Product Overview

---

## The Hook

**MediPay is the all-in-one pharmaceutical distribution management platform that eliminates payment collection chaos and inventory losses.** By automating post-dated check tracking, batch expiry management, and customer credit monitoring, pharmaceutical distributors reduce bad debt by up to 40% while cutting administrative overhead in half.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Intelligent Post-Dated Check (PDC) Management
**Stop losing money to forgotten checks and bounced payments.**

- Visual calendar and Kanban board views for all pending checks
- Automated reminders 7 days, 3 days, and day-of due dates
- Instant bounced check alerts to sales managers and finance teams
- Bank performance analytics to identify high-risk payment channels
- Full reconciliation workflow with settlement tracking

**Business Impact:** Collections teams report 30%+ improvement in on-time check deposits.

---

### 2. Batch-Level Inventory Control (FIFO/FEFO)
**Never write off expired pharmaceutical inventory again.**

- Automatic batch prioritization by expiry date
- 90-day expiration alerts for proactive stock rotation
- Damaged/sample quantity tracking per batch
- Real-time stock levels with critical threshold alerts
- Complete batch transaction history for compliance

**Business Impact:** Distributors typically save 5-15% of inventory value previously lost to expiration.

---

### 3. Customer Credit Intelligence
**Know your customers' payment behavior before extending credit.**

- Configurable credit terms and limits per customer
- Settlement delay scoring (10+ days, 20+, 50+, 100+ risk tiers)
- Historical payment pattern analysis
- Active/inactive status management with license tracking
- Order blocking for customers exceeding credit limits

**Business Impact:** Reduce bad debt exposure with data-driven credit decisions.

---

### 4. Role-Based Dashboards & Workflows
**Every team member sees exactly what they need - nothing more, nothing less.**

| Role | Dashboard Focus |
|------|-----------------|
| **Sales Rep** | Personal orders, customer pipeline, commission tracking |
| **Sales Manager** | Team performance, order approvals, customer health |
| **Finance** | PDC status, payment reconciliation, aging analysis |
| **Finance Manager** | Cash flow projections, credit risk, collection metrics |
| **Store Manager** | Dispatch queue, inventory levels, batch allocation |
| **Driver** | Delivery schedule, proof of delivery capture |

**Business Impact:** 50%+ reduction in time spent finding information across departments.

---

### 5. Automated Order-to-Cash Workflow
**From order creation to payment collection - fully tracked.**

```
Order Created → Approved → Invoiced → Dispatched → Delivered → Payment Collected → Reconciled
```

- Automatic status notifications to relevant teams at each stage
- Withholding tax tracking with document upload
- Partial payment and overpayment handling
- Customer balance management for credits/prepayments

**Business Impact:** Accelerate order fulfillment with zero status update meetings.
